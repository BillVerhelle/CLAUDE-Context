## ðŸ”‘ Key Strategic Pillars

### 1. **Systems of Action vs. Systems of Record**

**The Shift:**
- **Old Model:** Systems of record (core banking, lease accounting, CRMs) store and track what happened
- **New Model:** Systems of action execute in real-time at the moment of intent

**What This Means:**
- No more re-keying data
- No more switching between platforms
- No more manual policy interpretation
- No more employee-initiated workflows
- Everything happens **in real-time, borrower-initiated**

### 2. **Embedded Lending**

**Definition:** Financing integrated directly into the transaction itself, not downstream from it

**Impact:**
- SMB borrowers self-serve end-to-end
- Transactions complete instantly, 24/7/365
- No bank employee intermediation required
- Borrowers initiate, complete, and manage directly

**Current Problem:**
- Equipment finance still happens AFTER sales conclude
- Disconnected systems, manual handoffs, delayed decisions
- Employees process transactions while communicating via calls/emails
- Tracked in Salesforce and other systems of record
- This "internal busy-work" disappears with embedded lending

### 3. **AI Enhancement**

**Role of AI:**
- Enables decisions at point of action (not after review)
- Orchestrates workflows automatically
- Interacts directly with customers
- Triggers systems of record (not reverse)

**Combined Power:**
Together, embedded lending + AI = fundamental transformation

### 4. **The Structural Transformation**

**Cannot Be Incremental:**
- Requires abandoning employee-centric workflows entirely
- Not about replacing systems of record (they'll persist)
- Value creation shifts upstream to systems that:
  * Put borrowers online end-to-end
  * Initiate decisions
  * Orchestrate workflows
  * Interact directly with customers
  * Trigger systems of record
